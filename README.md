## â­ Features

- **Responsive Landing Page**: Includes a hero slider, top-rated plants, plant of the week, and green experts section.
- **All Plants Page**: Card view layout with sorting (price ascending/descending) and filtering by category and care level.
- **Plant Details Page**: Detailed view of plant info, including price, rating, stock, care level, provider, and consultation form.
- **Authentication**: Signup/Login with email & Google Sign-In; protected routes for plant details.
- **Promotional Section**: Engaging banner for discounts or seasonal offers.
